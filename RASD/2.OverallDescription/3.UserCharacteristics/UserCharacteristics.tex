	\section{User Characteristics}

		\paragraph{Users:}
		People having a smartphone with Internet connection and willing to report a \ic{Traffic violation} which has occurred. 
		\paragraph{Municipality:}
		Authorities having the possibility to receive \ic{Traffic violation} reports in order to generate \ic{Traffic tickets}. They can collect reports without being physically present and see all the details about a specific \ic{User report}. This also allow them to motivate any possible \ic{User report} discarded.
