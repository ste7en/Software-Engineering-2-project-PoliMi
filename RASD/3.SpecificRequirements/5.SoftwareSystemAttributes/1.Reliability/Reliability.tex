\subsection{Reliability\label{sect:3.5.1}}

The reliability of the system mainly depends on that of the Internet connection when it comes to sending data to SafeStreets and to the \ic{License plate recognition service}. Fault detection time must be kept minimum so as to promptly identify a fault and repair it as soon as possible.
